FAQs
Is CartMate a budgeting app?

No — it focuses on grocery clarity. Scan receipts, build smarter lists, and see simple spend insights. (Budgeting apps are great; CartMate simply solves the grocery problem well.)

Do I need to keep scanning forever?

No. Scans help build a reusable list and baseline. From there, maintain the list and scan weekly or whenever helpful.

What about privacy?

Your data is yours. We don't sell it. You can delete your account anytime from the Profile screen and it removes your data from our servers.
