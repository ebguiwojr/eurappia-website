Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your TravMate account:

  1. Log in to the TravMate app.
  2. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  3. Follow the confirmation steps to permanently remove your account and all associated data.

If you are unable to access the app, you can email us at support@eurappia.com with the subject “Delete My Account” from your registered email address.
