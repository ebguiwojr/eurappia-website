FAQ

How does AI support work in TravMate?

TravMate can draft trip outlines and answer questions through Trip Chat using your trip context. AI outputs are optional support and should be reviewed before final decisions.

Can I track expenses in different currencies?

Yes. Log expenses in local currency; TravMate stores a rate to your base currency so totals make sense later.

Is my data private?

Your trips, documents, and media are your own. You can delete any item any time from within the app.
