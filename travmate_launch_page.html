🧠 Frequently Asked Questions

What is TravMate?
TravMate is your personal travel companion — ideal for solo travelers, families, or groups. It helps you manage trips, bookings, expenses, and travel memories, all in one AI-enhanced app.

Is TravMate free to use?
Yes — during the beta, all features are free. After the beta ends, users will need a subscription, but all beta testers who leave feedback will receive free premium access.

What platforms does TravMate support?
TravMate is available on Android (via Google Play), iOS (via TestFlight), and Web. Mobile users can also upload photos/videos offline and sync later.

Can I upload booking documents?
Absolutely. You can attach PDFs, screenshots, or photos to any booking — TravMate uses these to generate AI-powered insights like itinerary suggestions.

What kind of AI insights will I get?
TravMate automatically generates weather summaries, transport tips, itinerary suggestions, and overall trip summaries — available in multiple languages.

Can I track expenses in different currencies?
Yes. TravMate supports multi-currency entry and auto-converts all expenses to your selected base currency using live or historical exchange rates.

Where can I get more help?
Check out the full in-app Help section under Profile > Help, or contact support@eurappia.com if you have questions.
