FAQs
Is TravMate a booking site?

No — it doesn’t sell flights or hotels. TravMate is your personal travel companion to organize everything in one app.

Do I need to enter every detail?

No. Start simple (one trip, one booking). Add more only if you want. The app works at any level of detail.

What about privacy?

Your trip data is yours. You can delete your account anytime from the Profile screen, and all your data is removed from our servers.
